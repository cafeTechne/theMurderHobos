PatElowin the lude plays bass in this traveling band of busybodies. He's usually scribin' tomes and pennin' essays with the hope of getting published and landing a tenure track position in Elvish Academia. He is a Philosopher first, and Bassist second. He has joined this merry band of adventurers and left his comfortable abode in the forests of old in search of a "cunning linguist" that recently knocked up his sister and skipped town with a travelling circus!

Nils the Monk has a penchant for Dwarven spirits and the course beards of Dwarven Lasses. Defrocked for his heretical antics and alcoholism, he roams from town to town working for food and drink. He plays trumpet and can swoon elven rangers with ballads of daring!

Lindellowyn earned her magical trombone in the Coliseum where she bested the beast of the East in a battle of wits.

To his ladies he's known simply as "Thorny" but he is Eregmoth "The Shadow Thorn", a skilled con-artist and thief. For several years he pocketed an exorbitant "Shipping and Processing Fee" while middle-manning warez by the Piraeus, but someone ratted him out. Now, he must make amends or else be burned alive in retribution. He's willing to work with almost anyone to find treasure, both to save his life and secure a good livelihood. He also plays trumpet and sings.

Hitta is a Nordic warrior of the North with a majestic mane. He recently joined the party after they helped him exact revenge on a snake oil salesman that was responsible for making his cousin sick. He, like Shandolf, enjoys hitting things.

Shandolf the Magnificent, or Shandolf the Shady as so many call him, is a first-rate sheister. His illusions are so good he has convinced HIMSELF that he is a real wizard (he's not.). He is also an expert on hitting things with sticks and also happens to be a foremost expert on African Swallows and is a surprisingly adroit singer!

Urg hates Goblins. Urg wields staff of shredding. Urg pretends to sing but really just talks loud.
